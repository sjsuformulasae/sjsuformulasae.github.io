WHO WE ARE

AND WHY WE RACE

In order to compete in Formula SAE, the Spartan Racing team designs, builds, and tests a new Formula style racecar each year based on regulations and standards similar to those imposed on professional motor sport teams. This exercise immerses students of all backgrounds in an environment that parallels that of a competitive work environment and professional race team. The multidimensional nature of the Formula SAE experience is an invaluable educational experience.

Why You Should Join

Internship and job opportunities for students are plentiful via team sponsorship and networking

FSAE provides practical supplements to theoretical coursework

Through this team you will broaden your learning experience by covering a wide range of engineering material

You will build ties between the SJSU COE and companies in Silicon Valley through FSAE
